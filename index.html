My Story

This curiosity led me to explore informatics and data science, where technology meets human-centered design. I became fascinated by how data can reveal patterns in behavior, improve systems, and create meaningful impact in education and society. I am still early in my journey, but I am deeply motivated to learn, grow, and build tools that help people.
